About Us

Prime Link Services is a Pakistan-based outsourcing company providing reliable, cost-effective backend support services to taxi companies across the United Kingdom. With a commitment to excellence, we deliver seamless communication, efficient dispatch handling, and round-the-clock operational support Founded with the vision to bridge the gap between affordability and quality, Prime Link Services is built on strong values: professionalism, dependability, and customer-centricity. Our team is composed of fluent English-speaking professionals with a deep understanding of UK taxi operations, including systems like Autocab365, iCabbi, and others. We serve as a true extension of your business — helping you scale effortlessly without compromising service quality. Whether you need support during peak hours, full-shift coverage, or 24/7 dispatching, we customize our services to meet your exact requirements.
